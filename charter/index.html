Welcome to the official doctrine of the Obsidian Empire, forged to guide our path through strife, shadow, and sovereignty.

👑 Emperor of Obsidian

The eternal flame that guides the Empire’s path. Wields final authority in all matters, ensuring balance between war, craft, diplomacy, and unity.

🔱 The Council of Four

Our empire thrives under the leadership of four officers, each representing a pillar of our strength.

⚔️ Warlord of Obsidian

The sword that cleaves chaos from order.

🎯 Role: Military Command, PvP Strategy, Node & Castle Sieges Responsibilities:
  • Lead all war efforts: sieges, open-world PvP, and guild wars
  • Train members in group tactics, counterplay, and battlefield synergy
  • Scout enemy threats and maintain readiness
  • Plan coordinated attack and defense strategies with allies

🛠️ Forgewarden

The fire beneath the mountain—silent, steady, and powerful.

🎯 Role: Crafting, Professions, Supply Management Responsibilities:

  • Oversee gathering and processing operations
  • Manage the Guild Crafting Planner & Resource Tracker
  • Track profession leveling and optimize artisan roles
  • Organize resource collection missions and caravan logistics

🕵️ Shadow Envoy

A voice in silence, a dagger unseen.

🎯 Role: Diplomacy, Intelligence, Reconnaissance Responsibilities:

  • Serve as the diplomatic voice of the guild
  • Maintain alliances, trade pacts, and war-time negotiations
  • Gather and report intel on rival guilds or node progressions
  • Scout enemy troop movement and guide caravan safety

🔥 Heartkeeper

The warmth of the hearth, the unity of the flame.

🎯 Role: Community, Recruitment, Events Responsibilities:

  • Welcome and guide new members into the guild
  • Organize internal events and downtime games (like Gladiator Arena!)
  • Keep morale high through celebrations, contests, and engagement
  • Mediate member conflicts and preserve a positive environment
